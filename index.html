What is it? A browser extension.

Philanthropist is a browser extension that helps make it easy to always direct some of the money you spend on Amazon to your favorite projects. If you are a fan of a project that happens to be a memeber of the Amazon Associate program, you can easily configure Philanthropist make sure that anytime you're browsing Amazon, your session is associated with that project.

Why use it? Support your favorite projects.

Many projects produce amazing work and offer it for free of charge. Sometimes their only source of revenue comes from the Amazon Associates program. You'll see links on their sites that take you to Amazon and if you buy anything after clicking through, they'll receive some money. This doesn't cost you anything extra, so why not do this for every purchase you make on Amazon? Well because sometimes you forget to go to your favorite project's site and click through their Amazon links, instead you go to Amazon directly. Philanthropist will make sure that even if you go to Amazon directly, any purchase you make will result in your favoirte project getting some money.

A list of known Amazon associates is maintained in Philanthropist's wiki on Github. Is your favoirte project missing form the list? Feel free to add it.

Where is it avaialble? In the Chrome Web Store.

Currently Philanthropist is only available for Chrome. To install it, go to Philanthropist's Chrome extension page. If you enjoy the extension, help make Philanthropist more visible by giving it a rating or a review.

A Firefox extension is in the pipeline. Check back here occasionally for updates.
